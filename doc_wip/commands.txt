Control:

:ack <jid>
:add <jid>
:del <jid>
:msg <jid> <text>
:pgp enc|dec|sig|chk <command>
:pwd <password>
# Priority at the beginning as it would be indistinguishable from the message.
:set [<priority>] available|chat|away|busy|xaway|invisible [<message>]
:set offline
# Implies ":add"
:sub <jid> [<message>]
# Implies ":del"
:usc <jid> [<message>]

Contact:

:ack
:add
:del
:msg <text>
:pgp key <keyId>
:pgp on|off
:sub [<message>]
:usc [<message>]
